About Us

We are a mobile application aiming to provide the best home nursing services with a skilled and experienced team of nurses licensed by the Nursing Syndicate.

Collection and Use of Personal Information:

Personal information that you enter within the application, including your location on Google Maps and phone number, may be disclosed to third parties within the scope of the requested services to enhance and improve the services. Personal information will not be shared beyond this limited scope.

We may record incoming phone calls to ensure better service, fulfill your requests accurately, and utilize them within the context of the nursing services provided by us. Your information will be retained for the necessary purposes and will not be used outside the scope of services.

Data Retention and Usage

We will retain the mentioned information as required for the purposes stated in the Privacy Policy. Your personal data will be used as necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our legal agreements and policies.

We will also retain usage data for internal analysis. Usage data is generally retained for a short period, except when used to enhance security, improve our service, or if required by law.

Your Rights

If you have provided any personal information on this website or left comments, you can request to receive an exported file of your personal data, including any data you have provided to us. You can also request the deletion of any personal data we hold about you.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update the Privacy Policy from time to time, and any changes will be effective upon publishing the new Privacy Policy on this page.
